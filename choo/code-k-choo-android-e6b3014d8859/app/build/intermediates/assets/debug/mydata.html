My Data Information

My Data Area
In this area you will find all personal data which are saved and therfore available for your next order. Only the data from your last order will be displayed here. In case of any of your data will change (e.g. new contact details or account number) you can easely do the changes here, so you will have the accurate information for oncoming orders. You can also make changes of your data in the overview section in the form process (just before submitting your form). Any data changes of a submitted form are not possible anymore.

Submissions
In this area you will find an overview of all submitted forms to Choo including the actual status of processing of your order.

WHow do we use your personla data?

Your personal data are necessary for the refund process. We will hand over the data to the Deutsche Bahn for further processing. Your Bank- and Account Information are necessary to transfer your refund amount.

Die Daten können jederzeit hier oder im Rahmen der Übersicht bei jeder Auftrags- anlage geändert werden.
