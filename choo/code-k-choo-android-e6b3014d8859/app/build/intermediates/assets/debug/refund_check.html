When you are entitled to compensation?

Regular Tickets
Starting with a 60 Min delay, you are entitled to a 25% refund of your fare. A 120 min delay entitles you for a 50% refund of your fare. Please note that in case you´ve bought a round trip ticket only the price for a single journey (50%) is used for refund calculations. 

ICE Sprinter surcharge paid?
Starting with a 30 min delay, you´re entitled to a full refund of the ICE Sprinter surcharge.

BahnCard 100
Starting with 60 Min delay you´re entitled to a flat reimbursement of 15 Euro for 1st Class and 10 Euro for 2nd Class BahnCard 100.

Long Distance Time Card
Starting with 60 Min delay you´re entitled to a flat reimbursement of 15 Euro for 1st Class and 10 Euro for 2nd Long Distance TimeCard.

Please note that the Choo App does not support Promotional Tickets as e.g. Happy Weekend Tickets, etc. or Short Distance Time Cards. Also, the German Bahn will refund an amount to an account only with a minimum refund of 4 EUR. For reimbursement claims with above mentioned limitations, please contact directly a German train service counter.

Reimbursement information of the Deutschen Bahn, February 2015
