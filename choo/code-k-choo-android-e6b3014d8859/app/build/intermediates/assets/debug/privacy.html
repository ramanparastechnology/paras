Privacy Information 

Choo is using your data in the following way

Collection, processing and use of stock data
We use your personal information solely for the purpose of establishing the contractual relationship to provide our services, to change and to design content. We use the data for advertising and market research only to the extent permitted by the statutory provisions.

Information concerning stored data
Upon written request, we will inform you about what data is stored about you in our house. We ask for your understanding, that in this respect, it is only possible to process a written request, signed by you.

Privacy
To protect personal data stored by us, we have taken extensive technical and operational safety precautions. These are regularly reviewed and - if necessary - adapted to new circumstances. All employees of our company have signed privacy policies. Breaching the data protection rules will result in significant sanctions. (e.g. the termination of employment).

Collecting and processing of data when using our website
If you visit the Choo website, our web server stores data temporarily for security reasons. Data that is temporarily stored is the domain name or IP address of your computer as well as the date on which you assume you access our site. Furthermore saved are file requests (file name and URL), the HTTP response code and the website from which you visit us, and the number of transferred bytes during the connection. Your name, your address or your phone number we can not grasp, unless this information is voluntarily left by you.

Social Plugins
We use so-called social plugins (hereinafter buttons) of social networks such as Facebook and Twitter. During your visit to our website, these buttons are disabled by default, ie without your intervention no data will be sent to the respective social networks. Before you can use the buttons, you need to activate them with a click. The button remains active until you deactivate them again.
After activation, a direct connection to the server of the respective social network is built up. The contents of the button is then transferred from the social networks directly to your browser and integrates it into the website.
After activating a button the respective social network can already collect data, regardless of whether you interact with the button. If you are logged in a social network, it can assign your visit to this site with your user account.
If you are a member of a social network and do not want this linked collected during your visit to our website with your data stored member data, you must log out before activating the buttons from the respective social network.
On the scope of the data that are collected by the social networks with their buttons, we have no control. Purpose and scope of data collection and further processing and use of data by the respective social networks as well as your rights and settings options for protecting your privacy please refer to the privacy policies of the respective social networks.

Contact
The protection and safety of our customers is of preferral importance to Choo. Should you have questions concerning data protection issues, contact Choo via e-mail under anfrage@chooapp.com.
