Question: Which types of ticket does Choo support?
Answer: Choo essentially covers all standard tickets which you can buy at Deutsche Bahn. It does not matter whether you purchase these tickets at a discount through a BahnCard25 or BahnCard50. Of course you can also make delays by purchased online tickets asserted. Also BahnCard100 and season tickets of long distance owners can make your delay claims. Rail & Fly tickets can also be submitted via Choo.

 
 
 
Question: Which types of ticket does Choo not support?
Answer: The following types of tickets you can not use with Choo: short distance time cards (Zeitkarte des Nahverkehrs), "Quer durchs Land Ticket", "Sch√∂nes Wochenend-Ticket". For these types of tickets only a lump-sum refund is granted in case of delay of up to EUR 2.25 by Deutsche Bahn. The German railway transfers but only from an amount of 4 EUR a refund. Please contact for a refund for these ticket types at a service center of Deutsche Bahn.

 
 
 
Question: Is Choo supporting also the reimbursement of overnight accommodation or taxi expenses in case of a delayed train?
Answer: follow-up costs that are incurred by a delay, can not be submitted via Choo. Please contact a service center of Deutsche Bahn.

 
 
 
Question: I have missed my connecting train and am thus belatedly. Does Choo support this case too?
Answer: Of course you can make the delay about Choo asserted. In the app there is a special search for this topic.

 
 
 
Question: My train is delayed because of a strike. Can I submit this to Choo also?
Answer: Yes, and delays that occur due to strikes may be submitted via Choo.

 
 
 
Question: The train was delayed due to a storm. Is that also a reason for compensation?
Answer: Yes, weather delays have to be reimbursed by Deutsche Bahn.

 
 
 
Question: The train was delayed due to a flooding. Is that also a reason for compensation?
Answer:  Yes, also weather-related delays have to be reimbursed by Deutsche Bahn.

 
 
 
Question: Do I get paid extras such as the Sprinter charge or for seat reservations?
Answer: Yes, the Sprinter surcharge, you already received a delay of 30 minutes to 100% refunded. The remaining fare you get proportionately also reimbursed. including without special services such as seat reservations.

 
 
 
Question: I bought my ticket with the BahnCard25 or BahnCard50. Will I get refunds too?
Answer:  Yes, you will get a reimbursement for the price you have paid for the fare.

 
 
 
Question: What is the refund amount with a 30 minutes delay?
Answer: You will just get the Sprinter surcharge refunded.

 
 
 
Question: What is the refund amount with a 60 minutes delay?
Answer: Starting at 60 min delay you will receive 25% of your fare for the trip refunded.

 
 
 
Question: What is the refund amount with a 120 minutes delay?
Answer:  Starting at 120 min delay you will receive 50% of your fare for the trip refunded.

 
 
 
Question: How long in retrospect, I may submit my ticket?
Answer: Choo will accept claims up to 6 month after the delayed train

 
 
 
Question: I set off in other European countries and arrived delayed at my destination in Germany. Does Choo supports that too?
Answer: If there is a train of Deutsche Bahn AG is, you can also submit this delay on Choo.
