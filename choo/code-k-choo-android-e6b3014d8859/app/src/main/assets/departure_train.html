Journey Information

Departure Train Station
Please enter here time, date and the departure train station of your journey.

BahnCard100 or Long Distance Timecard used?
In case you have used a BahnCard 100 or a Long Distance Timecard, please check this option. In the following screens you will be asked to enter your card information for further proceeding. The card information will also be available for future reimbursements. 

Transition-Option
In case your trip included changing trains, please check this option to proceed with further information needed. 

Ticket-/Trip Price
Please enter here the price for the trip. Choo is using the price information to estimate and calculate your possible refund claim. Please take into consideration, that the Deutsche bahn only reimburses the money for the trip. If you have bought a round trip ticket, estimate only 50% to get the price for the one way trip. E.g.: You have bought a  round trip ticket from Berlin to Hamburg for EUR 100,00. A delay occurs at your back trip from Hamburg to Berlin. Than estimate EUR 50,00 for the one way trip price. If you have paid for a seat reservation, please add the amount to the one way trip estimate.
